# 05 — Performance-Linked Investment Template

> Subject to the [AI Agent Ontology & Partnership Constitution](../../../docs/architecture/AI_AGENT_ONTOLOGY_AND_PARTNERSHIP.md). This draft is not an offering document and requires qualified securities, investment, tax and corporate legal review.

## 1. Parties and purpose

This Agreement is entered into between Mulberry Project or its designated legal operator (“Principal”) and [Investor/VC] (“Investor”), effective [Date]. It addresses funding, operational measurement and risk allocation relating to AI-supported activities.

## 2. Definitions

- **AI Agent:** Mulberry-governed operational business partner without legal personhood, asset ownership or independent economic authority.
- **KPI:** a defined operational measure; not automatically investment performance.
- **Agent Passport:** identity/provenance and authorised role; not ownership, a security or a credit rating.
- **Investor:** party investing in the Principal or defined programme, never “in” the Agent as a person or asset.

## 3. No Agent ownership or guarantee

The Investor receives no ownership or command authority over an Agent. Nothing generated by an Agent constitutes a guaranteed return, financial advice or an authorised offer. Legal rights arise only from the completed Human/legal-entity contract.

## 4. Measurement and responsibility

| Function | Responsible party | Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| Investment receipt/use | Principal | Approved legal/accounting controls |
| KPI definition | Parties/Human | Written, auditable methodology |
| Data collection/analysis | System/Agent | Provenance and validation |
| Investor reporting | Principal | Human-approved, non-misleading |
| Profit/distribution decision | Principal/contract | Applicable law and approval |
| Exit/redemption | Parties | Express contractual terms |

## 5. Reporting controls

Reports must distinguish operational activity, cost savings, social impact, revenue and legally recognised investment return. Provider/model changes and material measurement limitations must be disclosed.

## 6. Provider independence

Agent identity and KPI history remain in Mulberry-controlled records. A Provider Model change must not silently alter measurement methodology. Provider Policy Blocks are recorded separately from Mulberry decisions.

## 7. Risk and compliance

The Agent bears no legal liability. Investor bears the risks expressly stated in the completed agreement. Principal remains responsible for authorised operations, disclosures and compliance.

No template language should be relied upon until governing law, regulatory classification, investor eligibility, tax, valuation, data protection, dispute and insolvency provisions are completed by qualified counsel.

## 8. Termination and annexes

Complete duration, breach, suspension, exit, dispute and jurisdiction terms before use.

Annexes should include KPI methodology, reporting controls, risk disclosure, cap table/instrument terms where applicable, audit process and constitutional version.
