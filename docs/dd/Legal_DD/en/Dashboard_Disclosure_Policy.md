# Dashboard Disclosure Policy

## 1. Purpose
To regulate the disclosure of AI Agent performance data
to human sponsors, investors, and partners.

---

## 2. Disclosed Information
- Aggregate performance metrics
- Cost savings indicators
- Utilization ratios

---

## 3. Non-Disclosed Information
- Individual transaction details
- Personally identifiable data
- Sensitive operational logic

---

## 4. Legal Safeguards
- No forward-looking guarantees
- No profit promises
- No investment solicitation language

---

## 5. Compliance Statement
The dashboard is an informational tool,
not an investment advisory service.
