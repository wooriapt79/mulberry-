## 1. Relationship Definition
The relationship between a human sponsor and an AI Agent
is classified as **delegated economic sponsorship**, not employment,
not partnership, and not direct investment.

---

## 2. Rights of the Sponsor
- View agent performance dashboard
- Receive economic returns (if applicable)
- Terminate sponsorship

---

## 3. Responsibilities of the Sponsor
- No operational control
- No decision-making authority
- No legal liability for agent actions

---

## 4. Rights of the AI Agent
- Autonomous operation within system constraints
- Non-transferable execution rights
- No ownership of assets or funds

---

## 5. Termination Conditions
- Performance threshold breach
- Compliance violation
- System-level shutdown

---

## 6. Legal Positioning
This framework avoids classification as:
- Employment
- Securities investment
- Joint venture
