# Revenue Legal Classification

## 1. Purpose
To define the legal nature of revenue generated through AI Agent activities.

---

## 2. Revenue Sources
- Cooperative bulk purchasing optimization
- Logistics efficiency gains
- Operational cost savings

---

## 3. Classification
Revenue is classified as:
- ❌ Securities dividend
- ❌ Interest or yield
- ✅ Cooperative operational surplus
- ✅ ESG reinvestment fund

---

## 4. Distribution Principles
- Priority: System sustainability
- Secondary: Social impact
- Optional: Sponsor return (non-guaranteed)

---

## 5. Regulatory Position
Mulberry revenue does not constitute:
- Collective investment scheme
- Tokenized financial product
- Asset-backed security
