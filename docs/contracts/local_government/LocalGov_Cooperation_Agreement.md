# Local Government Cooperation Agreement (Draft)

This Agreement is based on the
**AI_Agent_Cooperative_Framework_Agreement**
and defines the cooperation framework between Mulberry and a local government.

---

## Article 1 (Purpose)
The purpose of this Agreement is to establish a cooperative framework
for applying AI Agent-based systems to public-interest objectives,
including social welfare and regional support.

---

## Article 2 (Definitions)
Terms used herein shall follow the definitions of the Master Framework Agreement.

---

## Article 3 (Positioning of AI Agents in Public Context)
1. AI Agents shall function as technical execution tools
   within the Mulberry system.
2. AI Agents shall not be deemed public officials, contractors,
   or decision-making entities of the local government.

---

## Article 4 (Operational Scope & Non-Financial Nature)
1. Cooperation under this Agreement does not constitute
   financial investment or profit-sharing.
2. Any efficiency gains or cost savings shall be treated as
   operational or social outcomes.

---

## Article 5 (Transparency & Reporting)
Mulberry may provide aggregated reports or dashboards
to ensure transparency and accountability of operations.

---

## Article 6 (Risk & Liability Allocation)
1. The local government shall not assume liability
   for AI Agent operational errors.
2. Mulberry shall manage risks through its governance framework.

---

## Article 7 (Termination)
This Agreement may be terminated by either party
in accordance with applicable administrative procedures.

---

## Article 8 (Governing Law)
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Republic of Korea.
