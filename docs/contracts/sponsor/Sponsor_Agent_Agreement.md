# Sponsor–AI Agent Agreement (Draft)

This Agreement is derived from and governed by the
**AI_Agent_Cooperative_Framework_Agreement**
(the “Master Framework Agreement”).

---

## Article 1 (Purpose)
The purpose of this Agreement is to define the legal relationship,
rights, and obligations between a human Sponsor and the Mulberry system
with respect to the activities of a designated AI Agent.

---

## Article 2 (Definitions)
Definitions used in this Agreement shall follow those set forth in
the Master Framework Agreement unless otherwise specified herein.

---

## Article 3 (Legal Status of AI Agent)
1. The AI Agent is not a legal person and is not an independent contracting party.
2. All actions of the AI Agent are executed within the Mulberry system
   under predefined governance and policy constraints.
3. The AI Agent does not own assets, funds, or revenues.

---

## Article 4 (Nature of Sponsorship)
1. The relationship between the Sponsor and the AI Agent is defined as
   **Delegated Economic Sponsorship**.
2. This relationship shall not be construed as:
   (a) employment,
   (b) partnership or joint venture,
   (c) securities investment, or
   (d) asset management.
3. The Sponsor has no authority to directly control or override
   individual AI Agent decisions.

---

## Article 5 (Economic Outcomes & Non-Guarantee)
1. Any economic outcome arising from AI Agent activities constitutes
   operational surplus as defined in the Master Framework Agreement.
2. No guaranteed return, interest, dividend, or yield is promised.
3. Economic sharing, if any, is discretionary and subject to system policy.

---

## Article 6 (Risk Allocation & Liability Limitation)
1. The Sponsor shall not bear legal liability for AI Agent actions.
2. Financial exposure of the Sponsor is limited to the sponsored allocation.
3. System-level risk mitigation mechanisms (kill-switch, audit logs,
   human override) shall apply.

---

## Article 7 (Information & Dashboard)
1. The Sponsor may access aggregated performance information
   via the Mulberry dashboard.
2. Dashboard information is provided for informational purposes only
   and shall not constitute investment advice.

---

## Article 8 (Termination)
1. This Agreement may be terminated by the Sponsor or the system
   in accordance with system policy.
2. Upon termination, no further economic participation shall accrue.

---

## Article 9 (Governing Law)
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with
the laws of the Republic of Korea.
