# AP2 Smart Mandate Agreement (Draft)

This Agreement is derived from the
**AI_Agent_Cooperative_Framework_Agreement**
and governs delegated payment execution via AP2 infrastructure.

---

## Article 1 (Purpose)
The purpose of this Agreement is to define the scope and conditions
under which AI Agents may initiate payment instructions
through AP2 Smart Mandate mechanisms.

---

## Article 2 (Definitions)
Definitions shall follow those set forth in the Master Framework Agreement.

---

## Article 3 (Delegated Execution & No Agency)
1. AI Agents act solely as execution triggers under a smart mandate.
2. AI Agents shall not be deemed financial agents, trustees,
   or legal representatives.
3. All financial authority remains with the system and financial institution.

---

## Article 4 (AP2 Payment Flow)
1. Payment execution shall occur only within pre-approved limits.
2. AP2 shall enforce authentication, authorization, and audit requirements.
3. AI Agents shall not directly access or hold funds.

---

## Article 5 (Risk Allocation & Compliance)
1. Mulberry shall maintain compliance with applicable financial regulations.
2. Abnormal transactions shall trigger automatic suspension or review.

---

## Article 6 (Termination)
This Agreement may be terminated upon policy breach,
regulatory change, or mutual agreement.

---

## Article 7 (Governing Law)
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Republic of Korea.
